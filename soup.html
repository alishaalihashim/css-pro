Soup Recipe


WINTER SPECIAL SOUP

Ingredients:

  • Chicken stock 6 cups
  • Chicken breast (chopped) 1
  • Salt ½ tsp + ½ tsp
  • White pepper ¼ tsp
  • Black pepper 1/2 tsp
  • Siracha sauce 1 tbsp
  • Hoi son sauce 1 tbsp
  • Chicken powder 1 tbsp (heaped)
  • Cabbage (finely chopped) 1/2 cup
  • Carrot (chopped) 2 tbsp
  • Mushroom (sliced) 2 tbsp
  • Spring onion leaves 1/4 cup
  • Coriander leaves (chopped) 2 tbsp
  • Corn flour 3 tbsp

Cooking Directions:

  1. Heat stock, add chicken and cook until chicken tender.
  2. Now add cabbage, carrot, mushroom, salt, white pepper, black pepper, siracha sauce, hoison sauce and chicken powder; cooks for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix corn flour in ¼ cup water and pour in soup to thicken.
  4. Lastly add coriander and spring onion leaf. Serve hot.
