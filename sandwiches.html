Sandwich Recipe


FRIED CHEESE SANDWICH

Ingredients:

  • Bread 8 slices
  • Cheese slices 4
  • Eggs 2 to 3 (beaten)
  • Mustard paste as required
  • Plain flour as required
  • Corn crumbs as required
  • Oil for fry

Cooking Directions:

  1. Remove crust from 8 slices of bread.
  2. Spread mustard paste on bread slices, place a slice of cheese on top. Cover with another slice of bread.
  3. Mix plain flour and corn flour in a plate.
  4. Dust sandwich lightly with flour, dip in beaten egg, roll in corn crumbs.
  5. Heal oil in frying pan and shallow fry till light golden.
  6. Cut into quarter, serve hot.
